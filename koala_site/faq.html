Koala FAQ

Koalas Fact Sheet

What does a koala look like?

Koalas have soft, wool-like fur that is gray above and white below. They resemble bears but are marsupials that carry young in a pouch.

What is the endangered status of koalas?

Koalas are classified as vulnerable.

How large can koalas get?

About 30 pounds and roughly 2 feet tall on average.

Where do koalas live?

Historically across Australia. Today mainly Queensland, New South Wales, Victoria, and South Australia.

What environments do koalas prefer?

Eucalyptus forests, coastal islands, and low woodlands.
